PAYROLL MODULE

User's Guide

Welcome to The PAYROLL Module. This Module contains the following links which you can make use of provided you are giving the privilege:

  • Enter Working Days

    • Here,we enter and get the no of days staff worked for the month to pay the staff based on the days he/she worked.

  • Staff Bank Details

    • This link takes care of the staff bank account details.

  • Compute Payroll

    • Here, We compute the salary for a chosen month.

  • Print Payroll Schedule

    • This link is for us to print our monthly payroll schedule for approval.

  • Print PaySlip

    • We use this link to print our monthly payslip for individual staff.

  • Schedule to Bank

    • This is the report we send to bank for corresponding posting to our staffs account.
