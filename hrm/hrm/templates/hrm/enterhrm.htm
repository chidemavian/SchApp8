HUMAN RESOURCES MODULE

User's Guide

Welcome to The Human Resources Module. This Module contains the following links which you can make use of provided you are giving the privilege:

  • New Staff Record

    • Here, new staff information is taking in to the system by fill in the appropriate textboxes provided.

  • Edit Staff Record

    • In case of any mistake or any new information about any staff, you click this link and put the name of the staff, click on his staff id and edit his/her record accordingly.

  • Staff Education

    • You click this link to enter any staff educational history or to amend in case of any mistake.

  • Professional Education/Body

    • This link is to append any Professional qualification to any staff.

  • Staff Report

    • Staff Report can be generated based on staff status, so this link is to generate such report.

  • Staff Individual Report

    • This link is to generate staff individual report.
